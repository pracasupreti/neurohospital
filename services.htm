Emergency Services

Our Hospital features a modern, 15-bed emergency room with a trauma team on standby and a section dedicated to resuscitation. A group of specialists, medical officials, certified nurses, and paramedics oversee this around-the-clock. Paramedics with extensive training and well-equipped ambulances are dispatched quickly by the Emergency Medical Services (EMS). The employees get routine certifications based on the most recent evidence-based procedures. When transporting severely ill patients, pre-hospital care is also provided by a specialist physician. EMS's objectives include not only getting patients safely to the closest hospital for immediate care, but also evaluating them and starting critical, perhaps life-saving care both on the spot and during the transfer.

The Wellness Center

Our Hospital's Wellness Centre provides a one-stop shop for all health screening needs as well as early disease detection and management. The first workup and evaluation is completed by a group of family physicians. A large selection of thoughtfully crafted health screening packages are available here. You will receive an overview of the various Health Packages from the Wellness staff.
