Dr. Birendra Kumar Bista

Senior Consultant Neurologist

Dr. Birendra Kumar Bista is the Chairman & Managing Director of Neuro Hospital Pvt Ltd. Birendra Kumar Bista is one of the first Neurologists in Nepal. He has been pioneering in the field of Neuroscience in Nepal and established the first neuroscience center in Eastern Nepal. He completed his study at Queen Square University, UK. Through the years the work of this neuroscience center has been recognized at home and abroad. He shows a keen interest in medical management providing state of art services to this impoverished region of Nepal. Recently he added the first stroke center in Nepal. He firmly believes in continuously updated education and its implementation in hospital practices.
